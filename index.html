I am a [Postdoctoral Researcher](https://uefconnect.uef.fi/en/person/lauri.peterson/) working on environmental politics and climate governance at the University of Eastern Finland. I am also a [Researcher](https://www.katalog.uu.se/profile/?id=N16-1504) at Uppsala University. I hold a PhD in Political Science from Uppsala University, a MA in International Relations and European Studies from Central European University and a BA in History from University of Tartu. In my PhD dissertation, I investigated climate change mitigation on the international, national and sub-national levels of governance by looking at domestic actors and institutions. My current research interests encompass the determinants of climate change mitigation, the role of climate hazards, and the political legitimacy of climate policies. I am currently engaged in several research projects, which investigate the drivers and barriers of the development of Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs), and attitudes towards climate action. My Google Scholar page can be found [here](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=-ipUeI0AAAAJ&hl=en) I am a Centre of Natural Hazards and Disaster Science [Fellow](https://www.cnds.se/about-us/fellows/) and a [Research Fellow](https://www.earthsystemgovernance.org/person/lauri-peterson/) Earth System Governance.
