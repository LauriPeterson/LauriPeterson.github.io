Welcome! I am a [Senior Researcher](https://uefconnect.uef.fi/en/person/lauri.peterson/) at the University of Eastern Finland, and a [Researcher](https://www.katalog.uu.se/profile/?id=N16-1504) at Uppsala University. I received my PhD in Political Science from Uppsala University in 2021 and my MA in International Relations and European Studies from Central European University in 2013.

In my PhD dissertation, I investigated climate change mitigation on the international, national and sub-national levels of governance by looking at domestic actors and institutions. My current research interests encompass the determinants of climate change mitigation, the role of climate hazards, and the political legitimacy of climate policies. 

I am a Centre of Natural Hazards and Disaster Science [Fellow](https://www.cnds.se/about-us/fellows/) and a Earth System Governance [Research Fellow](https://www.earthsystemgovernance.org/person/lauri-peterson/) .

I am currently engaged in several funded research projects, which investigate environmental politics and climate governance, including 2 Horizon Europe projects: [Achieving High-Integrity Voluntary Climate Action](https://achieveproject.eu/), [The Drivers and Barriers of the Development of Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs)](http://www.ndc-aspects.eu/), [The effects of climate hazards on climate-resilient development](https://mmw.wallenberg.org/mojliggor-naturkatastrofer-langtgaende-atgarder-klimatanpassning-och-hallbar-utveckling) and participates in three projects on how the welfare state shapes citizen attitudes to costly climate measures: [Climate Policy as a Social Question](https://www.statsvet.uu.se/research/climate-policy-as-a-social-question--can-social-policies-reduce-opposition-to-carbon-taxes-/) and [Can social policy reduce the risk of polarization over climate change mitigation](https://www.vr.se/english/swecris.html#/project/2023-01850_Formas), both funded by Formas, and [Do welfare systems shape public support for climate action? A cross-country experimental assessment]([https://www.statsvet.uu.se/research/climate-policy-as-a-social-question--can-social-policies-reduce-opposition-to-carbon-taxes-/](https://mmw.wallenberg.org/hur-kan-omfordelning-genom-olika-valfardssystem-oka-allmanhetens-acceptans-klimatatgarder)), funded by the Marianne and Marcus Wallenberg Foundation..  

My research has been published in *World Development, Ecological Economics, Global Environmental Politics, npj Climate Action, International Environmental Agreements: Politics, Law and Economics*, among other outlets.
